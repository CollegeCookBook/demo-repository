Snacks/Deserts
---------------

**14 meals**

**7 snacks**
Popcorn: Popcorn kernels can be popped on the stovetop or in the microwave, then seasoned with salt, butter, or your favorite seasonings.

Carrot and Celery Sticks with Hummus: Simply chop up some carrots and celery into sticks and dip them into hummus for a nutritious and filling snack.

Apple Slices with Peanut Butter: Slice up an apple and spread peanut butter on each slice for a delicious and satisfying snack that combines sweetness with protein.

Yogurt with Granola: Spoon some yogurt into a bowl and sprinkle granola on top for a quick and easy snack that's both creamy and crunchy.

Cheese and Crackers: Pair slices of cheese with crackers for a classic snack that provides a good balance of protein and carbohydrates.

Trail Mix: Combine nuts, dried fruits, and a few chocolate chips or M&M's for a customizable snack that's perfect for on-the-go.

Hard-boiled Eggs: Boil a batch of eggs at the beginning of the week and keep them in the fridge for a convenient and protein-rich snack option.

**7 Deserts**
Banana "Ice Cream": Peel and slice ripe bananas, then freeze the slices. Once frozen, blend them until creamy for a delicious and healthy "ice cream" alternative.

Rice Pudding: Cook rice in milk with sugar and your choice of flavorings like cinnamon or vanilla extract for a comforting and creamy dessert.

Chocolate Mug Cake: Mix together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and a little oil in a mug, then microwave for a quick and indulgent single-serving cake.

Fruit Salad: Chop up a variety of your favorite fruits and mix them together for a refreshing and naturally sweet dessert option.

Yogurt Parfait: Layer yogurt with granola and fruit in a glass or bowl for a simple and satisfying dessert that's also packed with protein.

Baked Apples: Core apples and fill them with a mixture of brown sugar, cinnamon, and butter, then bake until tender for a warm and comforting treat.

Peanut Butter Banana Bites: Slice bananas, spread peanut butter between the slices to make little sandwiches, then freeze for a quick and satisfying sweet snack.
